MAEMOD Mission

A short-term goal

To build capacity within the group and in the institutions/programmes of their collaborators through the training of new modellers and data analysts.

A long-term goal

To create a productive and sustainable collaborative modelling network in South East Asia which links with and supports the discipline in all tropical settings.
